Submitting Your Story

Before submitting a story please take a moment to read over and acknowledge the information provided.

Please note, by submitting a story you consent to and authourize my use of your written work submitted in this form. Stories that are considered will be used for audio adaptation.

You verify that you are the original creator of the written work and that it has not been plagiarised or based on any exsisting works or based in the universe of any popular brands.

Story Submission Guidelines

Take into consideration that it may take time for your story to be adapted to audio. If you have any questions on the stance of your submission feel free to message me on Twitter or my Reddit. If your story is accepted you will be notified via email.

Submissions with a word count under 100 will likely be rejected, short stories are preferably sent to my Reddit or via Twitter.

Please proof read and double check you submission prior to submitting to maximize your chance of having your story accepted. Check that your spelling, grammer and punctuation are correct as it can be difficult and confusing to read.
Please make use of paragraphs to make it easier to read and understand.

Stories that are considered not scary or do not relate to the themes of the channel will be rejected. Stories featuring excessive violence, sexual violence or gore will be rejected. Stories submitted in parts and are incomplete will also be rejected. If you wish to submit a full series or have plans to create a series please contact me via Twitter or Reddit.
